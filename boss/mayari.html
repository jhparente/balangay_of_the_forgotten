Mayari's Moveset

  • Moonlight Grace (Heal) :
    • Heal herself with a total of 25% max HP.
  • Lunar Strike (Single Target) :
    • Mayari will unleash a powerful strike, dealing 115% ATK + 20% MAG.
  • Moonfall Spear (Single Target, Debuff)
    • Deals 105% ATK
    • Inflict Moonfall: Reduces the hit enemies DEF by 20%
  • Tide of Night (Buff, Low AoE Damage) :
    • Mayari will be invulnerable next turn to all types of damage
    • Removes all the debuff.
    • Creates a mist that will damage all its opponent 30% to its current HP.
