About

Revered as the folk healer and spiritual guide, the Babaylan bridges the natural and the supernatural. Through rituals, chants, herbs, and sacred orasyon, they bring healing, blessings, and protection to the community. Beyond physical recovery, they also ward off illnesses and curses, safeguarding both body and spirit. In gameplay, the Babaylan’s role is crucial in sustaining allies, restoring health, and empowering the party with protective buffs.



Skill Cards (Moveset)

  • Heal – Restore HP equivalent to 100 (+50% MAG) to ally, and heals self for 50% of the amount, CD 2
  • Blessing – +20% dmg buff to ally (3 turns), CD 3
  • Purify – Removes all debuff to all players, CD 2
  • Sacrifice – Lose 200 HP, heal all allies HP equivalent to 150% -> 50 (+100% MAG), CD 3
