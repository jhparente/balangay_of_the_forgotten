About

The guardian and leader of the community, known in precolonial traditions as both a warrior and protector. The Bagani carries the weight of the tribe’s survival, standing as a bulwark against danger. With shield in hand, they absorb the heaviest blows and endure hardships so that others may thrive. In gameplay, the Bagani excels at defense and crowd control, drawing enemy attacks and shielding allies from harm.



Skill Cards (Moveset)

  • Shield Bash – Deals 100% ATK, 100% hit, -20 stamina, CD 1
  • Taunt – Boss targets you for 2 turns, CD 2
  • Fortify – Gain +300 temporary HP for 2 turns, -30 stamina, CD 2
  • Last Stand – If HP <20%, gain +50% defense for 3 turns, CD 4
  • Guardian's Oath – Sacrifice 100 HP, shield all ally for amount of 300, unstackable, CD 2
